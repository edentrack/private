# EDENTRACK – Farm Management App
## Complete Feature Guide & Benefits for Farmers

---

## What is EDENTRACK?

**EDENTRACK** is a farm management platform designed for poultry and livestock farmers. It replaces paper records and spreadsheets with a single digital system that tracks flocks, sales, expenses, inventory, tasks, and more. The app works on phones, tablets, and computers and supports multiple languages, including English and French.

**Primary users:** Poultry farmers (broilers, layers), livestock farmers, and farm managers who want clear records and better business decisions.

---

## How It Helps the Average Farmer

### Core Benefits

1. **Replace paper and scattered notes** – All records in one place, accessible from any device.
2. **Know if you’re making or losing money** – Track sales vs expenses and see profit in real time.
3. **Reduce losses from poor planning** – Alerts for vaccinations, low feed stock, and important tasks.
4. **Make data-driven decisions** – See trends in growth, spending, and sales.
5. **Save time** – Quick recording of daily activities (feed use, egg collection, sales).
6. **Work with a team** – Multiple roles (owner, manager, worker) with clear permissions.
7. **Offline-friendly** – Continue working without internet and sync when connected.

## Main Modules & Features

### 1. Dashboard – Farm Overview

**What it does:** Central view of farm status for the day.

**Key elements:**
- **Total birds** – Total animals across all flocks
- **Active flocks** – Number of flocks being managed
- **Pending tasks** – Tasks due today
- **Production cycle** – Phase and progress for each flock (e.g. growing, laying)
- **Daily summary** – Eggs collected, eggs sold, feed used, mortality, revenue, expenses, profit
- **Weight tracking** – Actual vs target weight for selected flock
- **Feed intake tracking** – Actual vs expected feed consumption
- **Water consumption tracking** – Actual vs expected water use
- **Inventory usage** – Fast daily feed/water usage recording
- **Today’s tasks** – Tasks to complete with quick completion
- **Quick egg collection** – Record eggs collected by size (for layers)
- **Cycle countdown** – Days/weeks until market or next phase
- **Spending overview** – Recent expenses and totals
- **Next vaccination** – Upcoming vaccination reminders
- **Recent activity** – Latest sales, expenses, and events

**Farmer benefit:** See at a glance what matters today without checking multiple notebooks or apps.

---

### 2. Flock Management

**What it does:** Manages groups of birds by arrival date, type, and breed.

**Features:**
- Create flocks (broiler, layer) with arrival date, breed, and initial count
- Track current count vs initial count (after mortality and sales)
- Filter by type (broiler/layer) and status (active/archived)
- Edit flock details (name, house/pen, notes)
- Archive flocks when sold or removed
- Flock switcher to quickly change between flocks

**Farmer benefit:** Clear record of each batch, its age, and how many birds remain. Easier planning of vaccinations, feed, and market dates.

---

### 3. Weight Tracking

**What it does:** Monitors growth against breed standards and targets.

**Features:**
- **Weight progress chart** – Actual vs target weight by week (broiler/layer standards)
- **Feed intake chart** – Actual vs expected feed use (g/bird/day)
- **Water consumption chart** – Actual vs expected water use (ml/bird/day)
- Log weights by week
- Edit feed and water records per week
- Performance indicators (e.g. % of target)
- Growth rate (g/week)
- Variance vs target

**Farmer benefit:** Spot slow growth or underfeeding early, adjust management, and improve market readiness and uniformity.

---

### 4. Mortality Tracking

**What it does:** Records and summarizes bird losses.

**Features:**
- Record mortality events with date, flock, and count
- Add cause (e.g. disease, predation, management)
- View mortality over time and by flock
- Mortality in daily summary

**Farmer benefit:** Track losses, see patterns, and justify changes in management or veterinary support.

---

### 5. Sales Management

**What it does:** Records bird and egg sales and manages customers.

**Features:**
- **Bird sales** – Record sales by flock, quantity, price per bird or per kg, total weight
- **Egg sales** – Record sales by size (small/medium/large/jumbo) or trays
- **Customer management** – Search by phone, auto-fill returning customers, add new customers
- **Invoices & receipts** – Create and send invoices, print receipts
- **Payment tracking** – Cash, mobile money, card, bank transfer; paid vs pending
- **Refunds** – Process refunds when needed

**Farmer benefit:** See who bought what, reduce pricing mistakes, and track revenue by flock and product.

---

### 6. Expense Tracking

**What it does:** Records all farm spending and links it to flocks and inventory.

**Features:**
- Record expenses with amount, description, category, and date
- Categories: Feed, Medication, Labor, Equipment, Utilities, Transport, Veterinary, Miscellaneous
- Link expenses to flocks (e.g. feed for a specific flock)
- Link purchases to inventory (feed, meds) so stock is updated automatically
- Add photos (e.g. receipts)
- Filter and search expenses
- Export reports (e.g. PDF)

**Farmer benefit:** Know exactly where money goes, compare costs across flocks, and see profit by batch.

---

### 7. Inventory Management

**What it does:** Tracks feed, medication, equipment, and supplies.

**Features:**
- Add feed types (name, unit, initial stock)
- Add other items (medication, equipment, supplies)
- Record daily usage (e.g. bags used today)
- Stock levels updated automatically when recording usage or purchases
- Configurable feed units (bags, kg, etc.) and weight per bag (e.g. 25 kg, 50 kg)
- Low stock awareness
- Hide items from dashboard
- Link expenses to inventory

**Farmer benefit:** Know what’s in stock, when to reorder, and avoid running out of critical items.

---

### 8. Tasks & Daily Operations

**What it does:** Manages daily and recurring farm tasks.

**Features:**
- **Today’s tasks** – List of tasks due today
- **Upcoming tasks** – Future tasks
- **Task history** – Completed tasks
- Task templates (e.g. feed pens, check water, vaccinate)
- Recurring and one-time tasks
- Assign tasks to team members (with team module)
- Link tasks to flocks
- Task limits per flock per day

**Farmer benefit:** Clear daily plan, fewer missed tasks (vaccinations, feeding), and better organisation.

---

### 9. Vaccination Schedule

**What it does:** Schedules and tracks vaccinations by flock.

**Features:**
- Vaccination schedule per flock and week
- Reminders for upcoming vaccinations
- Record completed vaccinations
- View schedule and history

**Farmer benefit:** Vaccinate on time, reduce disease risk, and improve flock health.

---

### 10. Analytics & Insights

**What it does:** Shows performance and financial trends.

**Features:**
- **Analytics dashboard** – KPIs, revenue, expenses, profit
- **Insights** – Trends, recommendations
- **Compare** – Compare flocks or periods
- **Reports** – Farm-level and flock-level summaries
- **Advanced metrics** – Growth, feed conversion, mortality rates
- **Financial summary** – Revenue, expenses, profit by period

**Farmer benefit:** Understand what works and what doesn’t, optimise inputs, and plan future cycles.

---

### 11. Team & Workforce

**What it does:** Manages team roles and permissions.

**Features:**
- **Roles** – Owner, Manager, Worker, Viewer
- **Permissions** – Control what managers and workers can access
- **Invite members** – Add team members by email
- **Activity log** – Who did what and when
- **Compensation** – Set pay for workers (with payroll module)

**Farmer benefit:** Delegate work safely, maintain oversight, and scale the farm with a team.

---

### 12. Shifts & Payroll

**What it does:** Manages work shifts and pay.

**Features:**
- Create recurring shifts
- Assign workers to shifts
- Payroll runs and adjustments
- Pay stubs
- Link labor to expenses

**Farmer benefit:** Clear shift schedules and wages, better labour cost tracking, and fair pay records.

---

### 13. Settings & Configuration

**What it does:** Configures farm and regional preferences.

**Features:**
- **Farm profile** – Name, country, currency
- **Currency** – XAF, CFA, USD, NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR
- **Egg settings** – Eggs per tray (e.g. 30)
- **Feed settings** – Feed unit (bags, kg, etc.) and weight per bag (e.g. 50 kg)
- **Growth targets** – Customise broiler/layer targets
- **Production cycle** – Broiler and layer phases and duration
- **Manager permissions** – What managers can do
- **Language** – English, French

**Farmer benefit:** Settings match local units and practices.

---

### 14. Marketplace

**What it does:** Connects farmers with buyers, suppliers, or services (if enabled).

**Farmer benefit:** Easier access to markets and inputs.

---

### 15. AI Assistant

**What it does:** Answers questions and gives recommendations based on farm data.

**Farmer benefit:** Quick answers and suggestions without searching multiple reports.

---

### 16. Helper Assistant

**What it does:** Provides guidance and tips in the app.

**Farmer benefit:** Easier onboarding and use for new users.

---

### 17. Smart Dashboard

**What it does:** Alternative dashboard layout with customisable widgets.

**Farmer benefit:** Focus on the information that matters most.

---

### 18. Activity Audit

**What it does:** Shows a log of changes and actions on the farm.

**Farmer benefit:** Transparency and accountability across the team.

---

### 19. Import (CSV)

**What it does:** Imports historical data from spreadsheets (CSV).

**Farmer benefit:** Move existing records into EDENTRACK without manual re-entry.

---

### 20. Notifications & Offline Support

**What it does:**
- Push notifications for tasks, vaccinations, low stock
- Offline mode – use the app without internet, sync later
- Real-time updates – data refreshes automatically

**Farmer benefit:** Works in low-connectivity areas and keeps the farmer informed.
